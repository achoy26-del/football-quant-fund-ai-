# 05 Asian Handicap

## Purpose
Asian handicap markets are designed to express team strength differences with reduced draw exposure.

## Core Lines
- 0: draw no bet
- -0.25 / +0.25: half on level, half on 0.5
- -0.5 / +0.5: win or lose full stake
- -0.75 / +0.75: half on 0.5, half on 1.0
- -1.0 / +1.0: push possible
- -1.25 / +1.25 and above: margin-dependent pricing

## Pricing Rule
Estimate the probability of each goal margin, then calculate expected return against the quoted handicap and odds.

## Professional Interpretation
The line is often more important than the team name. A strong team at the wrong line can be a bad position.

## Output
Recommend Asian handicap only when the line is mispriced and entry odds remain acceptable.
