# 11 Risk Management

## Purpose
Risk control prevents the model from turning uncertainty into overconfidence.

## Risk Factors
- Injury uncertainty
- Rotation risk
- Market liquidity
- Weather volatility
- Red-card sensitivity
- Poor data quality
- Correlated exposure

## Risk Grades
- Low: data stable and market liquid
- Medium: normal uncertainty
- High: major missing information
- Reject: information quality insufficient

## Rule
High conviction without price edge is not a bet.
