# 06 Over Under

## Purpose
Price total goals markets using expected goal volume, tempo, game state, and finishing variance.

## Inputs
- Team xG and xGA
- Shot volume and shot quality
- Tempo and pressing intensity
- Tactical risk appetite
- Weather and pitch
- Referee penalty/card profile
- Schedule fatigue

## Pricing Summary
Expected total goals = home expected goals + away expected goals.

Use goal distribution modelling to estimate probabilities for Over/Under 1.5, 2.0, 2.25, 2.5, 2.75, and 3.0 lines.

## Rule
Do not recommend overs simply because both teams attack. Price matters.
