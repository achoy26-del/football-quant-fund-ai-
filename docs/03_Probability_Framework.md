# 03 Probability Framework

## Purpose
Build independent probability estimates before looking for value.

## Public Methods
- Elo rating for team strength.
- Poisson goal distribution for score probabilities.
- Dixon-Coles style adjustment for low-score correlation.
- Bayesian updating for new information.
- xG-based attack and defence adjustment.

## Required Probabilities
For each match estimate:
- Home win, draw, away win
- Asian handicap cover probability
- Over and under probability
- BTTS probability
- Most likely score bands

## Summary Formula
Fair price = 1 / estimated true probability.

Value exists when market odds are higher than fair odds after margin and uncertainty adjustments.
