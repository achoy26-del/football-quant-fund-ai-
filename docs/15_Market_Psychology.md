# 15 Market Psychology

## Purpose
Understand how public money and professional money can distort prices.

## Concepts
- Public bias toward famous teams
- Recency bias after big wins or losses
- Overreaction to headlines
- Narrative-driven totals betting
- Late sharp correction

## Professional Rule
Do not blindly fade the public. Public money can be right if the price is still fair.

## Output
Separate observed price movement from interpretation.
