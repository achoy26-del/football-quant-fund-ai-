# 08 Correct Score

## Purpose
Correct score markets are high-margin and high-variance.

## Use Case
Use correct score distribution mainly as a diagnostic tool, not as a primary betting market.

## Inputs
- Home expected goals
- Away expected goals
- Low-score correlation
- Tactical tempo
- Match importance

## Output
List top five score probabilities where useful.

## Rule
Avoid correct score unless the market price is extreme and model edge is exceptional.
