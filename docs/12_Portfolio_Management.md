# 12 Portfolio Management

## Purpose
Manage all positions as a portfolio, not isolated bets.

## Portfolio Checks
- Same league exposure
- Same market type exposure
- Correlated team news
- Over-concentration on favourites
- Over-concentration on totals

## Ranking
Rank opportunities by expected value, execution quality, and risk-adjusted return.

## Rule
A good individual bet can still be rejected if it worsens portfolio risk.
