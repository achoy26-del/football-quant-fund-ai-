# 10 Bankroll Management

## Purpose
Bankroll management keeps the system alive through variance.

## Rules
- Define bankroll before analysis.
- Use fractional Kelly.
- Set daily, weekly, and monthly risk caps.
- Never increase stake to recover losses.

## Suggested Defaults
- Single position max: 2%
- Daily exposure max: 6%
- Weekly drawdown review: 10%

## Output
Every recommendation must include suggested stake and maximum acceptable exposure.
