# 04 Market Pricing

## Objective
Compare the model's fair price with the current market.

## Inputs
- Opening odds
- Current odds
- Closing line expectation
- Pinnacle and SBO prices
- Exchange liquidity
- Line movement

## Key Concepts
Bookmakers do not simply predict outcomes. They manage probability, margin, liquidity, and risk.

## Market Checks
- Has the line moved with information?
- Is the move sharp or public?
- Has value already disappeared?
- Is the best entry now, later, or live?

## Decision
Positive model edge is insufficient if execution price is poor.
