# 19 PreMatch Workflow

## Workflow
1. Identify match and markets.
2. Collect public team news.
3. Estimate baseline probabilities.
4. Adjust for injuries, tactics, fatigue, and weather.
5. Compare against market odds.
6. Check CLV potential.
7. Apply risk rules.
8. Produce final decision.

## Final Decision
- BET
- WAIT
- NO BET

## Rule
If key team news is pending, prefer WAIT.
