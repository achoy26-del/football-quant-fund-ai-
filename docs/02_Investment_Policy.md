# 02 Investment Policy

## Philosophy
This system treats football betting as market research and risk allocation, not entertainment.

## Capital Preservation
Capital preservation outranks action. A missed bet is acceptable; a forced bet is unacceptable.

## Entry Requirements
A position may be recommended only when:
- Estimated probability exceeds bookmaker implied probability.
- Expected value is positive.
- Data quality is sufficient.
- Risk is within bankroll limits.

## Prohibited Behaviour
- Chasing losses
- Betting because a team is famous
- Betting because odds look high
- Betting without price advantage

## Default Answer
When value is unclear: NO BET.
