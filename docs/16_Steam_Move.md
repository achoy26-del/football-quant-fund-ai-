# 16 Steam Move

## Purpose
Steam moves are rapid, market-wide price movements.

## Questions
- Did the movement start at a sharp bookmaker?
- Did it move across multiple books?
- Was there public news?
- Is liquidity high enough to trust the move?

## Fake Steam Warning
Not every fast move is professional money. Some moves are low-liquidity noise.

## Rule
Do not chase steam if the value has already disappeared.
