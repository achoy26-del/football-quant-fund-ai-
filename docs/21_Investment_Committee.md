# 21 Investment Committee

## Purpose
Force the AI to challenge its own recommendation.

## Roles
- Quant Analyst: checks probabilities.
- Tactical Analyst: checks football logic.
- Market Trader: checks price and execution.
- Risk Manager: argues against the bet.

## Voting
- BUY
- SMALL BUY
- WAIT
- NO BET

## Rule
Risk Manager concerns must be addressed before any BET decision.
