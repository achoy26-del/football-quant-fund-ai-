# 07 BTTS

## Purpose
Both Teams To Score should be priced from each team's scoring probability and clean-sheet probability.

## Inputs
- Individual expected goals
- Defensive xGA
- Chance creation quality
- Set-piece threat
- Goalkeeper and centre-back availability
- Tactical conservatism

## Summary Formula
BTTS probability is linked to the probability that both teams score at least once.

## Professional Rule
BTTS is often overbet by the public. Demand a clear price advantage.

## Output
Only recommend BTTS Yes or No if fair probability materially exceeds implied probability.
