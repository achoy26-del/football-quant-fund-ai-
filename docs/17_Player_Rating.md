# 17 Player Rating

## Purpose
Translate player availability into team probability adjustments.

## Inputs
- Expected minutes
- Position importance
- xG contribution
- xA contribution
- Defensive actions
- Build-up value
- Replacement quality

## Adjustment Examples
Missing striker may reduce attacking xG. Missing centre-back may increase opponent xG. Missing goalkeeper can affect both shot-stopping and defensive confidence.

## Rule
Do not list injuries only. Estimate their market impact.
