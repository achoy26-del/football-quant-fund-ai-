# 13 Closing Line Value

## Purpose
Closing Line Value measures whether the position beat the final market price.

## Why It Matters
Long-term professional bettors often judge process by whether they consistently beat the closing line.

## Inputs
- Entry odds
- Closing odds
- Market movement
- Information timing
- Liquidity

## Interpretation
Positive CLV suggests good price timing. Negative CLV requires review.

## Rule
A winning bet with bad CLV may still be poor process.
